Robot Printing Services

01

Custom End-Effectors & Grippers

Application-specific gripper fingers, vacuum cup arrays, and soft robotic graspers. Compliant mechanism grippers printed in TPU for delicate object handling. Rigid carbon-fiber nylon for high-payload industrial grippers.

02

Sensor & Camera Mounts

Cable-managed sensor mounting brackets for vision systems, force-torque sensors, and proximity detectors. Printable in carbon-fiber PETG for dimensional stability. Designed for specific robot wrist interfaces — UR, Fanuc, ABB compatible.

03

Structural Link Arms

Lightweight topology-optimized link arms for collaborative robot weight reduction. Composite nylon arms reducing moving mass by 30–50% vs aluminum — increasing payload capacity or reducing motor sizing requirements.

ROBOT MATERIALS

Materials for Robotics

Robot components demand a specific material profile — light weight for inertia reduction, rigidity for accuracy, or compliance for safe human interaction.

  • ● Onyx CF-Nylon — highest stiffness-to-weight ratio
  • ● PEEK — high-temperature, chemical-resistant housings
  • ● TPU 85A — soft robotic and compliant gripper elements
  • ● ASA — UV-stable exterior-rated robot housings
  • ● Aluminum-filled nylon — ESD-safe environments
  • ● Multi-material rigid-flex for integrated gripper-compliant tips
